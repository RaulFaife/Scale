Gameplay

The Game

ScaleVR

Our game gives players the ability to move around and explore different environments around them with different controls/movements. Gameplay includes the exploration of particles around them at a cellular level, the physical level at the environment of Florida International University's MMC campus, and the universal level in which players will get the opportunity to learn about the major solar systems and planets.
